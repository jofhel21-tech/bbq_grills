# BBQ Grill Website - Complete Customer Guide

Welcome to BBQ Grill! This step-by-step guide will help you navigate our website and place your first order.

## üöÄ Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Access the Website
1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
2. Type in the address bar: `http://127.0.0.1:8000` or `http://localhost:8000`
3. Press Enter - you'll see the login page

### Step 2: Create Your Account
1. Click the **"Register"** button on the login page
2. Fill out the form:
   - **Username**: Choose a unique username (e.g., "john_doe")
   - **Email**: Enter your email address
   - **Password**: Create a strong password
   - **Confirm Password**: Type your password again
3. Click **"Register"**
4. You'll be automatically logged in and taken to your dashboard

### Step 3: Browse Our BBQ Menu
1. Click **"Products"** in the top navigation menu
2. You'll see our delicious BBQ items with photos and prices:
   - BBQ Chicken
   - BBQ Pork
   - BBQ Hotdog
   - Isaw (Grilled Intestines)
   - Betamax (Grilled Blood)
   - And more!

## üõí How to Place Your First Order

### Step 1: Add Items to Your Cart
1. Browse the products page
2. Find something delicious (e.g., "BBQ Chicken")
3. Click the **"Add to Cart"** button
4. You'll see a green success message
5. Notice the cart icon in the top menu now shows "1"
6. Repeat for more items

### Step 2: Review Your Cart
1. Click **"Cart"** in the top navigation
2. Review your items:
   - Check product names and prices
   - Verify quantities
   - See the total amount
3. To change quantity: Update the number and click **"Update"**
4. To remove an item: Click **"Remove"**

### Step 3: Checkout Process
1. Click **"Proceed to Checkout"** button
2. Fill in your delivery information:
   - **Delivery Address**: Type your complete address (e.g., "123 Main St, Brgy. Poblacion, Naval, Biliran")
   - **Barangay**: Enter your barangay name (e.g., "Poblacion")
   - **Location Map** (optional): Click on the map to mark your exact location
3. Choose your **Payment Method**:
   - **Cash on Delivery (COD)**: Pay when food arrives
   - **GCash**: Pay via GCash mobile wallet
   - **Bank Transfer**: Pay via bank transfer
   - **Credit/Debit Card**: Pay with card
4. Add **Order Notes** (optional): Special instructions like "Extra spicy" or "No onions"
5. Review your order summary
6. Click **"Place Order"**
7. You'll get an order confirmation with your order number (e.g., "Order #123")

## üì± Managing Your Orders

### Viewing Your Orders
1. Click **"Orders"** in the navigation menu
2. You'll see all your orders with:
   - Order number (e.g., "#123")
   - Date ordered
   - Status (Pending ‚Üí Processing ‚Üí Out for Delivery ‚Üí Completed)
   - Total amount
   - Payment status

### Order Status Meanings
- **Pending**: We received your order and are reviewing it
- **Processing**: We're preparing your BBQ order
- **Out for Delivery**: Your food is on the way!
- **Completed**: Order delivered successfully
- **Cancelled**: Order was cancelled

### Tracking Your Order
1. Find your order in the Orders list
2. Click **"Track Order"**
3. See real-time status updates
4. View delivery location on the map (if available)
5. Check estimated delivery time

### Editing or Cancelling Orders
**You can edit/cancel orders ONLY if they are still "Pending" or "Processing"**

To Edit:
1. Find your order
2. Click **"Edit"**
3. Make changes to delivery address or notes
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

To Cancel:
1. Find your order
2. Click **"Delete"** or **"Cancel"**
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Order will be cancelled and refund processed (if applicable)

## üÜò Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Login Problems
**Can't remember password?**
- Contact admin for password reset
- Create a new account if needed

**Account locked?**
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Contact support if problem persists

### Cart Issues
**Items not adding to cart?**
1. Refresh the page (F5)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try logging out and back in

**Cart shows wrong total?**
1. Remove and re-add items
2. Refresh the page
3. Check if prices changed

### Order Problems
**Order not showing up?**
1. Check you're logged into the correct account
2. Wait 1-2 minutes for order to process
3. Check your email for confirmation

**Can't edit/cancel order?**
- Orders can only be modified if status is "Pending"
- Once "Processing" or "Out for Delivery", changes aren't allowed
- Contact us immediately for urgent changes

### Payment Issues
**Payment failed?**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify payment method details
3. Try a different payment method
4. Contact your bank if using card

### Delivery Problems
**Order taking too long?**
1. Check order tracking for updates
2. Normal delivery time is 30-60 minutes
3. Contact us if over 90 minutes

**Wrong delivery address?**
1. Call us immediately with correct address
2. Update your profile for future orders
