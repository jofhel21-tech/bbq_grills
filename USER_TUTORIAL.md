## Getting Started

### Accessing the Website
1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to: `http://127.0.0.1:8000` or `http://localhost:8000`
3. You'll be directed to the login page

---

## Registration & Login

### Creating a New Account
1. On the login page, click **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"**
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - Username (required)
   - Email address (required)
   - Password (required)
   - Confirm password (required)
3. Click **"Register"** button
4. You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard

### Logging In
1. Enter your **username** and **password**
2. Click **"Login"** button
3. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Logging Out
1. Click your username in the navigation bar
2. Select **"Logout"**

---

## Browsing Products

### Viewing All Products
1. From the navigation menu, click **"Products"**
2. Browse through available BBQ items with images and prices

### Searching for Products
1. Use the search bar at the top of the page
2. Type product name (e.g., "BBQ Chicken", "Pork")
3. Press **Enter** or click the search icon
4. View filtered results

### Filtering Products
1. On the Products page, use the filter options:
   - **Min Price**: Set minimum price
   - **Max Price**: Set maximum price
   - **Sort By**: Choose sorting option
     - Name (A-Z)
     - Price (Low to High)
     - Price (High to Low)
2. Click **"Apply Filters"**

## Shopping Cart & Checkout

### Adding Items to Cart
1. Browse products
2. Find a product you want
3. Click **"Add to Cart"** button
4. A success message will appear
5. Cart icon will update with item count

### Viewing Your Cart
1. Click **"Cart"** in the navigation menu
2. Review items in your cart:
   - Product name and image
   - Price per item
   - Quantity
   - Subtotal
3. View total amount at the bottom

### Updating Cart Quantities
1. In your cart, find the item to update
2. Change the quantity number
3. Click **"Update"** button
4. Total will recalculate automatically

### Removing Items from Cart
1. In your cart, find the item to remove
2. Click **"Remove"** button
3. Item will be deleted from cart

### Checkout Process
1. Click **"Proceed to Checkout"** in your cart
2. Fill in delivery information:
   - **Delivery Address**: Your complete address
   - **Barangay**: Your barangay name
   - **Location** (optional): Click map to set delivery location
3. Select **Payment Method**:
   - Cash on Delivery (COD)
   - GCash
   - Bank Transfer
   - Credit/Debit Card
4. Add **Order Notes** (optional)
5. Review order summary
6. Click **"Place Order"** button
7. You'll receive an order confirmation with order number

## Managing Orders

### Viewing Your Orders
1. Click **"Orders"** in the navigation menu
2. See list of all your orders with:
   - Order number
   - Date placed
   - Status (Pending, Processing, Completed, Cancelled)
   - Total amount
   - Payment status

### Viewing Order Details
1. In the Orders list, click on an order number
2. View complete order information:
   - Items ordered
   - Quantities and prices
   - Delivery address
   - Payment method
   - Order status

### Editing an Order
1. Find the order you want to edit
2. Click **"Edit"** button
3. Modify order details (only if order is still pending)
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

**Note**: Orders cannot be edited once they are out for delivery or completed with successful payment.

### Cancelling an Order
1. Find the order you want to cancel
2. Click **"Delete"** or **"Cancel"** button
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Order will be cancelled

**Note**: Orders cannot be cancelled once they are out for delivery or completed with successful payment.

## Making Reservations

### Creating a Reservation
1. Click **"Reservations"** in the navigation menu
2. Click **"Create New Reservation"** button
3. Fill in reservation details:
   - **Date**: Select reservation date
   - **Time**: Choose time slot
   - **Number of Guests**: Enter guest count
   - **Special Requests** (optional): Add any special requirements
4. Click **"Submit Reservation"**
5. Confirmation message will appear

### Viewing Your Reservations
1. Click **"Reservations"** in the navigation menu
2. See all your reservations with:
   - Date and time
   - Number of guests
   - Status (Pending, Confirmed, Cancelled)

### Editing a Reservation
1. Find the reservation to modify
2. Click **"Edit"** button
3. Update reservation details
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Cancelling a Reservation
1. Find the reservation to cancel
2. Click **"Cancel"** or **"Delete"** button
3. Confirm cancellation

## Tracking Your Order

### Accessing Order Tracking
1. Go to **"Orders"** page
2. Find your order
3. Click **"Track Order"** button

### Understanding Tracking Status
- **Order Placed**: Order received and confirmed
- **Confirmed**: Order is being prepared
- **Out for Delivery**: Order is on the way
- **Delivered**: Order has been delivered
- **Cancelled**: Order was cancelled

### Viewing Delivery Location
1. On the tracking page, view the map
2. See current delivery location (if available)
3. Check estimated delivery time

## User Profile & History

### Viewing Your Profile
1. Click your username in the navigation bar
2. Select **"Profile"**
3. View your account information:
   - Username and email
   - Total orders placed
   - Total reservations made
   - Total amount spent

### Viewing Activity History
1. Click **"History"** in the navigation menu
2. See all your activities:
   - Orders placed
   - Products viewed
   - Cart actions
   - Reservations made
   - Login history

### Deleting History Entries
1. In the History page, find an entry
2. Click **"Delete"** button next to it
3. Confirm deletion

### Clearing All History
1. In the History page, click **"Clear All History"**
2. Confirm you want to clear all entries
3. All history will be deleted

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Login?
- Check username and password spelling
- Use "Forgot Password" if needed
- Ensure account is registered

### Cart Not Updating?
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try logging out and back in

### Order Not Showing?
- Check "Orders" page
- Ensure you're logged in with correct account
- Wait a moment for order to process

### Can't Edit Order?
- Check order status
- Orders in delivery or completed cannot be edited
- Contact admin for assistance
